Captain Stealth (Stealth, Puzzle)

Captain Stealth is a top-down stealth game with puzzle elements. 
You play as Captain Stealth whose task is to guide your client, a prisoner, out of the prison. 
Avoid guards and security cameras while collecting keys to progress through rooms. 
Distract guards to lure them away from their patrol path!
